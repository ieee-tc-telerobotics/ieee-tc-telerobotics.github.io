Welcome to the IEEE Technical Committee for Telerobotics Webpage

The TC for Telerobotics is focused on

  • Promoting the research on telerobotics and teleoperation and its various applications,
  • Supporting the organization of conference workshops/tutorials/special sessions and journal special issues,
  • Facilitating the exchange of news and sharing of research data among its members.

  • An issue of the terminology "master and slave" (from co-chairs of TC Telerobotics)
    • As the IEEE TC for Telerobotics, we recommended:
    • 1. That the terms “master” or “slave” are not used in future publications and presentations to describe components of a telerobotic system.
    • 2. The choice of alternative terms is left to the discretion of individual researchers. Various terms may be appropriate for different teleoperation scenarios; suggestions from the community have included “haptic device/remote robot”, “leader/follower”, "driver/driven", and "teleoperating/teleoperated”.
