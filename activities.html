Activities

Robotics For Combating COVID-19:

COVID should serve as a catalyst to reconsider, at a fundamental level, the paradigms of healthcare delivery and how more extensive, targeted, and deliberate use of technology can reduce the loss of human lives and can alleviate the burden on the healthcare staff.

Ongoing Activities:

  • Recruitment of new members: We have increased the number of TC members from 43 to 78 after advertising the TC on social media.
  • Continuing to encourage and support TC members to organize workshops, special issues, etc.
  • Enhanced social media presence: Creating pages for the TC on LinkedIn, Twitter and YouTube as news sharing platforms.
  • Creating a page for the TC on ResearchGate as a publication sharing platform.
  • Creating a page for the TC on GitHub as a data sharing platform.

Sample of Other Activities:

  • Keehoon Kim serving as Editor (responsible for recruiting about 25 Associate Editors) for IROS 2017-2019.
  • Mahdi Tavakoli serving as Workshop Co-Chair in World Haptics Conference 2021.
  • Both Keehoon Kim and Mahdi Tavakoli serving as guest technical editors for concurrent submissions to IEEE Transactions on Haptics and World Haptics Conference 2021.
  • Mahdi Tavakoli serving as Program Co-Chair for International Symposium on Medical Robotics in 2019-2021.
